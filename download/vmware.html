MidnightBSD community virtual appliance

This MidnightBSD appliance is based on 0.1.1-RELEASE. It is 222MB compressedand around 700MB uncompressed. Only the root account is configured and there is no password. DHCP is enabled. X.org 6.9 and perl are installed.
