MNBSD-2025-4: tcpdump DOS

Severity: Unknown

Affected Package: tcpdump

Summary: tcpdump DOS

Description

The ppp decapsulator in tcpdump 4.9.3 can be convinced to allocate a large amount of memory. A remote attacker may be able to cause a denial of service.
