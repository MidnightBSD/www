When you use our Products or Websites, you may be asked for personal identifying information such as name,or email address. (primarily for the bug tracker)

In addition, when installing MidnightBSD, you are asked if you wish to install the bsdstats installation reporting tool. That tool tracks your computer via a unique identifier and is a third party service. You may wish to visit their website to find out more about what information is tracked.

The MidnightBSD package installation tool downloads packages from our server and may log your IP address and operating system version.

If you wish to withdrawl consent, please email webmaster@midnightbsd.org with the relevant details.

By giving us such information, you will need to consent to our using it in the manner described in this policy.

Third-party advertisers may also create and access cookies, which will be subject to their privacy policies – we accept no responsibility or liability for the use of such third parties’ cookies. If you do not wish cookies to be placed on your PC or handheld device, then they can be disabled in your web browser. The option to do so is normally found in your browser’s “security settings” section. Please note that permanently disabling cookies in your browser may hinder your use of our Websites as well as other websites and interactive services.

You can also install ad blocking software to limit the use of your information getting sent to third parties.
