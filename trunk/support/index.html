Developer Blog

During initial planning stages, support for MidnightBSD is limited. It is recommended that you view the FreeBSD® Handbook.

All FreeBSD® security advisories have been patched in MidnightBSD as of October 2007.

If you need to report a security issue with MidnightBSD, contact Adam Karim at security@midnightbsd.org.

E-mail questions@midnightbsd.org with any support questions that you may have. You can also use our forums and mailing lists.
